# Phase 4 Plan 02: Industry Limit Enforcement Closure

## Objective
- **What:** Replace the current placeholder `industry-limit` logic with real projected concentration enforcement.
- **Why:** `RSK-01` is not satisfied while industry rules only consist of blocklist handling plus a no-op concentration rule.
- **Output:** A real `industry-limit` gate with service and CLI regression coverage.

## Tasks

<task id="04-02-01" type="code">
  <name>Implement projected industry concentration enforcement</name>
  <files>src/risk/service.rs, src/risk/models.rs</files>
  <action>Use existing account snapshots and industry resolution to compute current and projected exposure for the target industry, then reject buys when the configured threshold would be exceeded. Preserve fail-closed behavior when industry data required for enforcement is unavailable.</action>
  <acceptance_criteria>
    <item>`industry-limit` no longer behaves as a logging-only placeholder.</item>
    <item>Projected concentration is compared against the configured threshold before buy approval.</item>
    <item>Failure messages remain operator-actionable.</item>
  </acceptance_criteria>
</task>

<task id="04-02-02" type="test">

  <acceptance_criteria>
